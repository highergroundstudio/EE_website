about us.

Elevated Equities offers a simple, quick commercial loan solution to creative Asset Based loans and more. Our average turn time from start to funding is 10 days to 2 weeks. Each month we have access to about $20 to $40 million in capital to place. Collateral types range from land to development projects to owner-occupied to farm & ranch to income producing properties. LTV ratios range from 25% to 90% depending on the type of property.

Our core business is true bridge loans to get the job done and help the borrower move on to the next lower cost loan or sell for a profit. We also leverage our relationships to provide our clients that next lower cost take-out loan, which improves the performance of our portfolio. As the simplicity and speed increase, thus the risk increases which in turn increases the interest rate and points. Our closed transactions are a testament to our proven track record.
